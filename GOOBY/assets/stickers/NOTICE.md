## Stickers

All 36 achievement sticker images (4 pages × 9 stickers) are original first-party artwork generated for Gooby's Cozy Burrow. No third-party art is used. The deterministic build pipeline is `scripts/stickers/process.py`; per-sticker master/runtime SHA-256 hashes, byte counts, grid provenance, and bilingual alt text are recorded in `src/data/stickers/manifest.json`.

- License: original first-party artwork generated for Gooby's Cozy Burrow
- Masters: `assets/stickers/masters/` (512×512 PNG, alpha-corrected)
- Runtime images: `public/assets/stickers/` (256×256 transparent WebP)
