# Family PWR — Power & Charge Network (`power_grid`)

Global sequence 23–46 · 24 features (21 core / 3 stretch) · core wave W5, stretch W15 · catalog family string: `power_grid`

Generation, storage, transmission and metering built on the charge graph introduced by U04/U05/U19. Everything downstream (SHD, TES, TUB, RAIL, GOL, WEA, EXO) draws from this family. Baseline Cg values are defined in INDEX.md "Vocabulary"; the numbers below are the binding initial balance constants (tunable only via `cuprum-common.json5`; gametests read the same config).

**Economy formulas (binding):** normal passive baseline **B = 5 Cg/t** (the output of one standard unattended generator under its normal condition: water wheel on 3-wide flow, thermo on lava+water, windlass at y≥128 clear, stage-3 solar at noon). Jar fill: 100,000 Cg ÷ 5 Cg/t = **20,000 ticks = 16 min 40 s** (inside the 10–20 min design window). Lightning capture: 270,000 Cg ÷ 5 Cg/t = **54,000 ticks = 45 min** of baseline — one strike equals 45 minutes of passive generation, making storm capture the premium source. Consumers deliberately draw from **storage** at rates far above B (machines are burst loads); sustained industry needs generator banks (outputs stack linearly) or storm capture.
**Balance constants:** U19 wire 200 Cg/t throughput; **line-loss model (binding):** delivered charge falls linearly by 2 percentage points per full 16-block span on bare U19 wire and by 0.5 percentage points per full 16-block span on HV lines, clamped at 0% delivered (so a 128-block run = 8 spans = exactly 84% delivered bare, exactly 96% delivered HV); bus bar 400 Cg/t; insulated cable 150 Cg/t; strike deposit 270,000 Cg; Leyden Jar (U05) 100,000 Cg; small cell 25,000 Cg.
**Performance budget:** graph solver ≤0.15 ms/tick at 1,000 nodes; idle generators sleep (no ticker) and wake on graph demand events; catenary spans render batched at ≤0.1 ms/frame per 50 spans.
**Persistence contract:** the per-dimension charge graph is SavedData; node charge, breaker/balancer settings and fuse states survive chunk unload, reload and server restart; unloaded sub-islands freeze (no phantom transfer).
**Security contract:** all GUI writes (balancer tiers, breaker reset) are C2S payloads validated for ≤8-block distance and rate-limited to 4/s per player.
**Cross-family interfaces:** exposes the `ChargeNode` block-API lookup consumed by every machine family; PWR-16 is the only redstone↔charge bridge; PWR-10 introduces the global per-day wind vector consumed by WEA-07/WEA-08 and DEC-09; PWR-18 priority tiers are consumed by SHD-07.
