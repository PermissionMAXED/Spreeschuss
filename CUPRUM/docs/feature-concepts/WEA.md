# Family WEA — Weather & Sky Systems (`weather_sky`)

Global sequence 143–156 · 14 features (10 core / 4 stretch) · core wave W9, stretch W15 · catalog family string: `weather_sky`

Forecasting, wind, storm harvesting and sky spectacle around U21 and U04. Honesty rule: vanilla weather is global per dimension — "local" effects (hail, heatwave, aurora) are bounded field simulations plus client visuals, and the handbook says so. **WEA-03 and U21 are the mod's two explicit dimension-global weather actions** (an INDEX-sanctioned exception to the bounded-radius rule); everything else in this family is radius-bounded.

**Balance constants:** forecasting is information, not power; the cloud seeder (WEA-03) is a cheap one-shot precursor to U21; fulgurite (WEA-06) charge shards are consumed by TOOL-08 and QOL-11 recipes, making natural storms worth chasing before capture infrastructure exists.
**Security contract:** weather-changing actions (WEA-03 rocket, U21 modes, WEA-14 hail) are server-config gated (`weather_control_enabled`), require permission node `cuprum.weather.use` when a provider exists (else OP level 2), are server-authoritative, and are logged with the actor's UUID. WEA-03 additionally enforces a 36,000-tick (30 min) per-dimension cooldown.
**Performance budget:** the wind vector is one global daily computation (introduced by PWR-10) that WEA consumers only read; gust events affect only loaded chunks within 64 blocks of a player; aurora/hail visuals honor QOL-04 reduced-effects caps.
**Cross-family interfaces:** barometer/siren states feed PWR-16 redstone; fulgurite is an OXI-smeltable block; WEA-12 reads the same strike scheduler U04 uses; GEN-11 aurora zones grant an efficiency bonus to WEA-13 projectors (optional synergy defined in GEN-11, no recipe gating in either direction).
