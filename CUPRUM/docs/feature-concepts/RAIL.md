# Family RAIL — Mag-Rails & Transport (`mag_transport`)

Global sequence 109–122 · 14 features (11 core / 3 stretch) · core wave W8, stretch W15 · catalog family string: `mag_transport`

Carts, stations, signaling and freight on top of U10's hovering mag-rails. Movement is server-authoritative spline motion on the custom MagCart entity; vanilla minecart physics is never inherited. RAIL-10 owns its own Route Card item contract (waypoint list codec); it is unrelated to GOL-03 golem punch cards, which belong to a later wave.

**Balance constants:** mag cruise speed up to 24 blocks/s (3× the ~8 blocks/s of powered rail); boosters add 4 blocks/s per pass at 100 Cg; elevator lifts cost 500 Cg per 32-block ascent; freight sleds carry 3 stacks so tubes stay optimal for bulk and rails for distance plus passengers.
**Performance budget:** cart entity tick ≤0.03 ms; sections without carts do zero signaling work; station displays update at 1 Hz.
**Persistence contract:** carts, their cargo manifests, signal section occupancy and station schedules persist across chunk unload/reload/restart; a cart frozen in an unloaded chunk resumes with identical position, velocity and cargo.
**Security contract:** route programming and station schedule edits are C2S-validated (≤8-block GUI distance, 4/s rate limit); boarding gates mount only players who stand in the gate zone themselves.
**Cross-family interfaces:** RAIL-06 sleds auto-dock to TUB ports and chests; RAIL-08 signals expose comparator occupancy for redstone builders; RAIL-12 reuses the station name registry of RAIL-04.
